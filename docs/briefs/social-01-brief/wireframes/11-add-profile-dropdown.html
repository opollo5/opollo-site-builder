Add-profile dropdown

Triggered by the "All profiles" button in the filter bar. Two-stage dropdown: first level shows "Add profile" (which expands the right-side sub-menu), second level lists all available connections with their brand icons. TikTok carries the "new" badge using --color-brand-green. Each item routes to /company/social/connections/connect/:platform.
