# Acme Corp Website Brief

A quick overview of what we need.

## Home

The home page is the front door of the site. We want a hero with the tagline "Build better websites faster" followed by a short subhead. Under that there should be three feature cards with icons, each describing one of our main value props: speed, flexibility, and support. Below the cards, include a testimonials strip with two or three customer quotes. Close with a final call-to-action section pointing people to the free trial. Keep the tone confident but warm, and avoid marketing jargon. The header should stay fixed on scroll and include links to About, Pricing, Docs, and a "Start free trial" button in the primary color. The footer should have four columns: Product, Company, Resources, Legal. Include company address, social links, and a brief copyright line. The home page is the first impression, so aim for a balanced layout that is neither too dense nor too airy. Think Stripe meets Linear in aesthetic terms, with generous whitespace and a clear visual hierarchy that leads the eye from the hero through the value props to the CTA. Feature-card icons should be outlined (not filled) and monochromatic, and the testimonials should include a small circular avatar, the quote, the name, and the role+company on a single line.

## About

Tell the story of the company — why we started, who we serve, what we believe. Start with a one-line mission statement near the top. Include a team section with five headshots and short bios. Mention that we're a remote-first team spanning four time zones. Close with a short "What's next" paragraph hinting at upcoming product work. The tone should be personal and specific; avoid cliches about "revolutionizing" anything.

## Pricing

Three tiers: Starter, Pro, Enterprise. Starter is free, Pro is $29/month, Enterprise is custom pricing. Emphasize Pro as the recommended choice. Include a comparison table listing what's in each tier — seats, support SLAs, integrations. Add an FAQ below the pricing cards. Include questions about cancellation, billing, trial length, and data export.

## Integrations

List every integration we support. Group them into categories: CRM, Analytics, Communication, Dev Tools. Each integration should have a logo, short description, and a "Learn more" link. Include a search box at the top.

## Contact

Simple form with name, email, company, and message. Also show an office address and a support email. Keep it short.
